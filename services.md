We design experiences that nurture harmony between cats, dogs, and the humans who love them. Choose a package or mix and match modules to fit your household.

### Studio Programs

- **Agility Labs** — Structured drills and video analysis for competitive duos.
- **Companion Confidence Coaching** — Small-group sessions focused on desensitization, cooperative care, and enrichment swaps.
- **Paw-litical Roundtables** — Facilitated conversations to resolve roommate stalemates with humor and empathy.

### On-Site Consults

- **Home Harmony Audit** — Floor plan review, feeding station setup, and traffic flow adjustments.
- **Custom Play Design** — Install modular climbing walls, scent gardens, or interactive feeders tailored to your pet crew.
- **Health Sync** — Coordinate vet checklists, medication reminders, and shared wellness logs for multi-guardian households.

### Creative Services

- **Content Studio** — Photo, video, and podcast production to celebrate your pets' story.
- **Event Production** — Pop-up arenas, projection mapping, and live commentary for showdowns big or small.
- **Workshop Licensing** — Train your team to facilitate our signature programs in your community.
